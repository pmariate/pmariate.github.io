ElBidets

ELBidets assists users in locating restrooms within UPLB classrooms. It offers search functions, filters for accessibility and cleanliness ratings, and allows users to provide reviews and ratings. These filtering options cater to diverse needs, while user reviews contribute to a dynamic platform, sharing firsthand experiences. This community-driven approach fosters collaboration and enriches the user experience.Framework used is MERN.
